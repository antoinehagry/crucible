About · who's behind Crucible

Run by Antoine Hagry.

Crucible is a one-person practice. Every validation is run by me, end to end — no junior desk, no outsourcing, no reselling your rules.

Duke University UIUC 2 yrs · ES futures

I trained at Duke University and the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, and I've spent the last two years trading ES futures systematically — living the exact gap Crucible exists to close.

I built Crucible after watching too many strategies — my own included — pass a clean backtest and then quietly bleed out in live. The culprit is almost never the idea; it's the execution the backtest never modelled: real fills, queue position, clustered drawdowns.

So I built the process I wished I'd had — reconstruct the system against order-level ES data, and judge it on survival, not on a flattering curve.

Confidentiality

What you send me stays with me. I don't redistribute, reuse, or trade your rules — and I'll sign an NDA before you share anything if you prefer. It's just me on the other end.
